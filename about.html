What is Vikora?

It is an online career mentor Platform where students can get a path to their Career.

Why is it needed?

Assume a student wants to become a Photographer and in their surroundings everybody doesn't know about how to become a Photographer and what is the right path. student research on the internet. We all know the internet has a huge amount of data by different Persons on their experiences and research data. Student is stuck with huge amounts of data. At this time Vikora help Student by providing a roadmap to achieve their Career.

Key features of Vikora

  • Get the right and best roadmap for your career.
  • Virtual Career Experience (VCE)
  • Talk to Experts of their Career.

What is Virtual Career Experience?

Virtual Career Experience (VCE) It provides an experience and type of work to students before pursuing their Career.

What is Talk to Experts of their Career?

Talk to Expert of their Career this feature clears the doubt of students where students can talk to Experienced experts of their Career
