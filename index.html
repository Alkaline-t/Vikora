    Artificial Intelligence (AI)

    What is AI?
    Artificial Intelligence refers to computer systems that perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, understanding language, vision, and decision-making.

    Advantages

    • Automates repetitive or dangerous tasks
    • Processes vast amounts of data quickly
    • Enables new technologies such as self-driving cars, medical diagnosis, and smart assistants
    • Reduces human error

    Disadvantages

    • Potential job displacement due to automation
    • High initial development cost
    • Can have biases if trained improperly
    • Raises ethical and privacy concerns

    Career Opportunities

    • AI/Machine Learning Engineer
    • Data Scientist
    • Robotics Engineer
    • AI Product Manager
    • Research Scientist
    • Natural Language Processing Engineer
